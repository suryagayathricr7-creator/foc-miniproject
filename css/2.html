MICROSOFT

Microsoft is a leading global technology company founded in 1975 by Bill Gates and Paul Allen. It is well known for developing the Windows operating system and the Microsoft Office suite of applications. The company also provides cloud computing services through Microsoft Azure, which is widely used by businesses around the world. Microsoft has expanded into hardware products like the Xbox gaming console. Its headquarters are located in Redmond. Under the leadership of Satya Nadella, the company focuses on innovation in artificial intelligence and cloud technologies. Microsoft continues to play a major role in shaping the modern digital world.
